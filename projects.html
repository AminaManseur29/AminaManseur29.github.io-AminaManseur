Diffusion for Trajectories

Generating financial time series from generative AI models (diffusion models), with the aim of augmenting data where financial data is scarce and expensive.
Used S&P500 and Bitcoin price data to train and evaluate models. Main challenges:

  • Exploration of 1D diffusion models for signal generation
  • Evaluation of signal quality using standard and custom metrics
  • Focus on financial signal properties: volatility clustering, heavy tails, no return autocorrelation

Applied Statistics Project

Forecasting electric vehicle electricity consumption in France using mobility projections.
Based on the 2019 INSEE “Enquête Mobilité des Personnes” survey.
Focused on two scenarios:

  • Demographic change (INSEE projections for 2040–2070)
  • Global warming (projections from Météo France and CNRM)
Method: resampling and calibration with the Calmar algorithm.

Bayesian Time Series Clustering

Replication and application of Fröhwirth-Schnatter & Kaufmann (2008).
Bayesian model-based clustering for multiple time series using finite mixture models and MCMC simulations.
Applied to GDP data to jointly estimate clusters, cluster-specific parameters, and the optimal number of clusters.
